# Retail Pulse: Advanced Business Intelligence Dashboard 📊

## Project Overview
**Retail Pulse** is a comprehensive, 12-page interactive business intelligence solution built from scratch in Power BI. Designed to analyze a robust dataset of over 100,000 retail transaction records, this project transforms raw operational data into actionable strategic insights for executive decision-making.

The dashboards maintain a strict, professional visual hierarchy—utilizing a dark-themed canvas, strict conditional color logic (Green for Revenue, Blue for Profit), and dynamic cross-filtering to allow users to isolate performance by region, time, product, and customer cohort.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Tools
* **Data Visualization & BI:** Power BI
* **Data Transformation:** Power Query
* **Calculations & Measures:** DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* **Complementary Skills:** Python, SQL (Data Extraction & Validation)

## 📈 Key Dashboards & Features
1. **Executive Summary:** High-level overview of total business health (₹2.51B Total Revenue, 100K Total Customers).
2. **Sales & Trend Analysis:** Multi-year revenue growth tracking and seasonal peak identification.
3. **Product Performance:** Profitability mapping to isolate core revenue-driving inventory (e.g., Beds & Refrigerators).
4. **Customer Analytics:** High-value segmentation and geographic performance mapping across India.
5. **Inventory Risk:** Supply chain vulnerability tracking to identify high flight-risk stockouts.
6. **Master Interactive Filter:** A dedicated control panel allowing global cross-filtering across all 12 pages via Sync Slicers.

## 💡 Business Insights Generated
* **Customer Retention:** Identified a 100% repeat customer rate, alongside a strict 90-day churn risk threshold.
* **Profit Efficiency:** While May generates the highest gross volume, July yields the highest net profit margin due to optimized product mixes.
* **Regional Equality:** Proven market penetration with revenue split almost evenly across Tier 1, Tier 2, and Village demographics.
